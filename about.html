What is the Internet of Things?

IoT is the next big advancement in the internet, where everyday objects are given network connectivity.

What is the IoT Open Innovation Lab?

The IoT Open Innovation Lab is an organization at Northeastern University focused on enabling students, faculty, and staff with resources to help them develop new use cases and applications for the Internet of Things. The resources we offer are numerous, ranging from hardware to hackathons with grand prizes - all at your disposal.
